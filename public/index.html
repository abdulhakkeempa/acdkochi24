F.A.Q

  • What is AWSUGKOCHI??

    AWS User Group Kochi (AWSUGKOCHI) is a Kochi based group united by community-led learnings and diversified by the wide variety of tools and technologies closely knit with Amazon Web Services, Cloud Computing Technology, AWS Design Implementation and Servicing, High Scalability / Performance Computing, Production use cases of AWS, Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence and much more.

  • Who is conducting this event?

    The AWS Community Day is supported by AWS but independent of them. This event is organized and managed by AWS User Group Kochi with help from AWS and other User Groups from India.

  • How can I join AWSUGKOCHI?

    AWSUGKOCHI membership is through Meetup group, Join us via http://meetup.com/awsugkochi/

  • Can I present a topic in the Community Day?

    Yes, you are welcome to come forward and present topics. Please contact us and we will allocate a slot for you.

  • Is it free, how to register for the event?

    This is not a free event like our meetups. We will be charging a minimum amount from the attendees to cover the hotel expenses.

  • Can I cancel my registration?

    Cancelation is not allowed, we will not be processing any refunds. However, you can transfer the ticket to someone else. Contact us if you would like to transfer your ticket to someone else in case if you are notable to attend the event.

  • Do we have walk-in registration?

    No, all attendees should register in advance. Tickets will be closed by the first week of October based on availability.

  • Additional questions?

    We are always happy to hear from you. You can reach us for sponsorship enquires, topic proposals, ticketing, feedback or any general question on AWS Community Day Kochi 2022, please drop an email to info@awsugkochi.in
